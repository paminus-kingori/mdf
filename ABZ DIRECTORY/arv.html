ABZ Acronyms

ARV – Antiretroviral drug

These are HIV drugs because HIV is a type of virus called retrovirus. ARVs do not kill the virus, rather, they block different stages of the virus’s life cycle. By doing so, the virus is unable to replicate and make copies of itself. If treatment continues without interruption, the viral population will drop to a point where it is undetectable (meaning not zero but below the level of detection with current testing technologies)
